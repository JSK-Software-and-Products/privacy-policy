Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Mini Games ("MiniGameHub", "the App") is developed by Hang ("we", "us", "our"). This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for your name, email address, or any other identifying information.

Game Center

The App integrates with Apple Game Center to provide leaderboards and achievements. Your Game Center profile, scores, and achievements are managed entirely by Apple. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how Apple handles this data.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases (e.g., extra lives, coins, power-ups). All transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Apple's policies for details on purchase data handling.

Local Data Storage

The App stores your game progress, high scores, coin balance, and preferences locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The App does not currently use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since we collect no personal data from any user, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
